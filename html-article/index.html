President Dallin H. Oaks Shares Four Ways to come Closer to Jesus Christ

In a BYU devotional, the prophet encourages faith, humility, seeking trusted help, and patience amid spiritual challenges



In a speech at Brigham Young University (BYU) — his first public address as President of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints — Dallin H. Oaks shared four ways to come closer to Jesus Christ. At the weekly campus devotional address on Tuesday, February 10, 2026, President Oaks first emphasized a well-known admonition that his predecessor, President Russell M. Nelson, shared in April 2018.
